Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not currently integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly before any such integration is activated.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
